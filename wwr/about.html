Down The River

About Us

At Down The River, we are passionate about delivering safe, exciting rafting experiences for all skill levels. We believe every adventure should be safe, exciting, and unforgettable. Our experienced guides are passionate about the outdoors and dedicated to creating memorable rafting experiences for beginners and thrill-seekers alike. Whether you are looking for a peaceful scenic float or an adrenaline-filled rapid, we are here to make your journey extraordinary.

History

Down The River was founded in 2001 with a simple mission: to share the beauty and excitement of river rafting with everyone. What began as a small family-run operation with just two rafts and a handful of passionate guides quickly grew into a trusted local adventure company. From the beginning, safety, professionalism, and unforgettable experiences have been at the heart of everything we do. Over the years, we have expanded our routes to include beginner-friendly scenic floats, intermediate adventures, and challenging rapids for experienced thrill-seakers.

Over the years, our team has grown to include certified instructors, safety specialists, and outdorr enthusiasts who share a deep respect for nature and teamwork. We continuously train our staff to ensure that every guest feels confident and supported on the water.. Today, Down The River proudly serves families, friends, schools, and corporate groups looking to connect through adventure. While our equipment and facilities have modernized over time, our core values remain unchanged: integrity, safety, and a passion for the river. We are proud to be part of our comunity and look forward to guiding many more unforgettable journeys for years to come.
